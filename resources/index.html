Metrics & Evaluation

Here are some links to useful writing considering the fraught nature of measuring anyone or anything, and how it becomes particularly complicated when attemping to measure the work (or "impact") of grassroots organizations.

  • Kathryn Moeller's (2019)'s The Ghost Statistic that Haunts Women's Empowerment, The New Yorker
  • Jerry Muller's The Tyranny of Metrics (Princeton Universtiy Press, 2018)
    • ...and Stefan Collini's (2018) Kept Alive for Thirty Days review of it.
  • Steffen Mau's The Metric Society: On the Quantification of the Social, (Polity, 2019)
  • Steven Teles & Mark Schmitt (2011)'s The Elusive Craft of Evaluating Advocacy, Stanford Social Innovation Review
